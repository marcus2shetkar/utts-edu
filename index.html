History

"Go work today in my vineyard" (Mat: 21:28)
As per the vision given by the Almighty God, Millennium Baptist Church was established by Dr. K.J. Vijaya Kumar in the year 2001 on July 1st. After establishing the Church we were able to construct the Church building and parsanaze within 2 years. Later as per the will of God and with the resolution of all the members of Millennium Baptist Church, we started 1 year Certificate of Theology course prayerfully on 11th August 2003 being inaugurated by Rev. Dr. S. Joseph, the former principal of A.C.T.C, Hyderabad, India. The certificate course C.Th was started in the church premises on residential basis in 2003. In 2008 miraculously God gave us opportunities to get affiliation to United Theological Research University, Secunderabad, India. We formulated our own bye-laws and constitution and have been running the Theological Seminary under the name "UNITED THEOLOGICAL SEMINARY SOCIETY" in Kurnool City, Andhra Pradesh, India. Our seminary is autonomous and independent.

About Us

We offer admissions on inter-denominational basis and we are happy to say that different churches (including Baptist Churches, RCM Churches, Independent Churches and Anglican Churches) are sending their members to our seminary for theological studies. We give training to students having duration of 1 year and 2 years depending upon their educational qualifications. We also offer this training to Pastors to upgrade their qualifications and to the students who obtained some theological certificate. We are extremely happy to say that employees in Government services and private services and business people are undergoing the theological training in our seminary. We praise Almighty God for the blessing given to the students of our seminary to get opportunity to work in various churches as Pastors. It is as knowing to note that around 902 students acquired their graduation with in the period from 2003 to 2018.
Our Seminary is Accredited to UTRUSR (Secunderabad, Telangana ) recognized by Berkely University, California.
We have pleasure to share with you that the candidates from other states also completed the Theological studies and serving god with vision.

Aims & Objectives

Following are our Aims & Objectives:
1) To train men and women who have a genuine call for evangelistic and pastoral ministries.
2) To prepare men and women to lead a life of spiritual and academic excellence that would make them future leaders of the church and society.
3) To provide the students various opportunities for ministry both inside the college and among the neighboring rural and urban communities.
4) To help students to discover and explore their God given gifts and talents and encourage them to translate into preaching, singing, music, writing, arts painting etc at the same time being sensitive to the needs of the people, especially the poor and the needy.
5) To provide the church in India with the dedicated and talented indigenous leadership who would transcend all the denominational boundaries and trigger the outreach deeper into the society.
6) To train and encourage the students to become aware of the pluralistic religious and cultural context of our country for effective communication of the Gospel.
7) To give ordinations who have 2 years of pastoral experience in the ministry of Jesus for both men and women.
8) To help for the nurture and nourishment of the spiritual development of the church and society.
9) To promote the Christian program and to formulate broad policies for the co-operation efforts of the churches.
10) To support Christian programs of religious education and medical services and to build and operate church buildings, chapels, hospitals, dispensaries, schools, colleges, orphanages, hostel homes for the aged and any other similar institutions of services of relief and development for the poor and needy without discrimination as it is charitable and non-profitable organization/society.
11) To promote co-operation with Christian churches and organizations for evangelism and mission.
12) To promote fellowship and network in any protestant theological college and seminaries and related Christian organizations.
13) To confer and award the degrees like C.Th, G.Th, B.Th, B.D, M.Min, D.Min, D.D, D.Th and P.Hd (for both internal and external).
14) To continue library and reading room for the students and to those connected with the society.
15) To provide, maintain and run boarding house and residential accommodation for students and to those connected with society without profit motive.
16) To spread and propagate spiritual development and spiritual thinking among the un-reached millions especially in rural and slum areas of urban.
17) To construct the churches, community halls under the minority control for spiritual and mental development.
18) To implement the minorities schemes, aides, projects with the help of funds allotted by the Government.
19) To conduct revival meetings, seminars, retreats and family get-together programs, children camps, bible clubs and community development programs.
20) To support pastors, bible women and who ever in the ministry of jesus by raising funds from the donors.
We believe that God would satisfy all our needs through his abundant grace. We invite you to be part of these sacred programs by extending your financial support.

Curriculum

The Curriculum of our seminary is as mentioned below:

For one year course every student should study 10 subjects, write 30 assignments, attend Seminary Classes, and undergo Practical work in one Church for 3 months. He must write Annual Examination in 10 subjects and obtain pass marks. Then only there will be awarded the required certificate.

For two year courses every student will be required to study 20 subjects, write 60 assignments, attend Seminary Classes, and undergo Practical work in one of the allotted Churches for 3 months. He should write the 1st year Annual Examinations in 10 papers and secure pass marks. Then only the candidates will get the certificate.

For 3 year course, subjects and assignments are based on type of course.
